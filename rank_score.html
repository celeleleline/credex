What is a Rank Score?

Rank Score is used for university entrance and course selection. It's calculated from your best credits across multiple subjects.

  • Excellence credits = 4 points each
  • Merit credits = 3 points each
  • Achieved credits = 2 points each
  • Literacy/Numeracy credits don't count toward rank score

Your rank score is calculated from your actual credits in the system.
